Toy Company Sales Dashboard

PowerBI Project
Toy Company Sales Dashboard


The project involves analyzing the sales performance of a toy company for the year 2023. The main goal is to understand the factors driving the year-over-year increase in total revenue by examining product and store performance.

Customer Requirement / Problem Statement:
The toy company needs to:
1. Connect to and transform sales data for visualization in Power BI.

2. Analyze sales performance to identify key drivers of revenue growth.

3. Ensure the data is presented in a clear, user-friendly manner.


Soution:

1. Data Transformation and Modeling: Use Power Query to connect to data sources, normalize data into smaller tables, merge tables, and create calculated columns for total cost, revenue, and profit. Establish relationships between tables in Power BI's model view.

2. Visualization and Analysis: Prepare the Power BI report page with a background image, create and organize DAX measures, apply conditional formatting, and analyze product and store performance using various visualizations. Include geographic and inventory analysis with advanced DAX calculations.

3. Sales Forecasting: Calculate average daily sales and project future sales to determine if the sales target will be met, using interactive features like adjustable goals and scenario analysis for dynamic forecasting.
